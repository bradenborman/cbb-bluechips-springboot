College Basketball's Bluechips

Wave goodbye

to busted brackets

The winner will be crowned shortly after the championship game has been completed and will be awarded to the person with the highest net worth. Each person who plays, is tasked with the challenge of turning $100,000 of fake cash, into the highest amount they can. During the tournament, a total of 67 games will be played over a few weeks span. Each of those games is an opportunity to increase your net worth.

This is a completely free to play game with the top 10 players receiving a prize totaling $490 in all.

How to be

The 2020 winner of CBB Bluechips

The winner will be crowned once the championship game has been played and will be awarded to the person with the highest net-worth. The way to do this, is to purchase shares of teams throughout the tournament who beat the point spread. Each game played, one team will have their market value increase while the other will result in loosing some value.

Portfolio

Getting the winners in your portfolio

While on the portfolio page, you can see a list of all the teams you currently are vested in. You can see the Amount Owned, Current Market Price per 1 Share, and a Sum of what the Total Value of that team would be.
